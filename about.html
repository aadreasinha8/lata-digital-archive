About the Project

This digital archive is a student-led digital humanities project that documents and analyses the songs of Lata Mangeshkar across decades, languages, and themes. The project aims to explore how her voice shaped emotional expression and cultural memory in Indian cinema.

Our Aim

The archive combines metadata, historical context, and thematic categorisation to present Lata Mangeshkar’s work as both a musical and cultural phenomenon.
